The Concrete Lines series examines the geometric abstractions that emerge from modern urban architecture. Shot exclusively during high-contrast morning and evening light, the photographs emphasize the intersections of shape, shadow, and building materials.

By isolating specific corners, joints, and facades, the series highlights the sculptural quality of modern concrete architecture, turning functional buildings into structural abstract art.
