Captured along the rugged, windswept Atlantic coast. Using a long exposure of 120 seconds, the restless movements of the waves are smoothed into a soft, dreamlike fog, while the dark, volcanic basalt rocks remain sharply defined.

The resulting image acts as a study in contrast—between the transient, fluid nature of water and the permanent, solid presence of stone. The print features exceptionally deep, velvety blacks and soft, luminous highlights.

Each print is individually signed and numbered by the artist on the lower border, and is accompanied by a Certificate of Authenticity with a serialized security hologram.
