The *Helvetic Geometries* series is a conceptual architectural catalog exploring the graphic, sculptural quality of modern structural forms in Switzerland. Focusing on brutalist curves, parallel glass towers, and concrete spirals, the photographs highlight how functional buildings interact with light to become abstract geometries.

By presenting these landmarks in stark, high-contrast black and white, the series removes temporary urban distractions, emphasizing raw form, line, and volume.
