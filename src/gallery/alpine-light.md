The Alpine Light series was captured over three winter seasons in the Swiss cantons of Glarus, Uri, and Graubünden. Operating in freezing conditions with mechanical medium-format cameras, the project is a deliberate exercise in slow, meditative photography.

By isolating solitary huts and mountain peaks in high-contrast compositions, the series explores the emotional resonance of alpine stillness. The landscapes are stripped of temporary human presence, focusing purely on geological scale and atmospheric depth.
