The Silent Coastlines series explores the boundary lines where water meets stone. Using long exposure exposures ranging from 30 seconds to several minutes, the series captures the smoothing of wave turbulence, rendering oceans into soft mists and emphasizing the static permanence of rock formations.

The series is presented in high-contrast monochrome, focusing the viewer’s eye on texture, volume, and composition.
