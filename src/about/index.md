## Biography

Ars Photographica is the creative playground of Martin Splitt, a Zurich-based photographer dedicated to capturing the light of this world. Working with analog and digital photography, the work explores themes of space, life, and the subtle textures of light.

Rather than documenting reality, the goal is to distill the sensory experience of a moment into a physical print that invites quiet contemplation.

> "Photography takes an instant out of time, altering life by holding it still." - Dorothea Lange

## The Art of Printmaking

Every photograph presented in the portfolio is printed directly by the artist in their studio. 
The entire process is part of the art for me - from taking the picture to printing in the darkroom or making a zine, the art is the entirety of it.

## Experimentation and joy

The whole journey from the thought of capturing a moment all the way to printing in the darkroom or folding a zine is full of joy and experimentation. Trying new processes, different chemicals or an unusual approach to things, it's all part of my art.
